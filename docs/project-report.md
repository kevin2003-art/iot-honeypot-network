## 1. Introduction

Healthcare environments rely heavily on connected IoT devices such as patient monitors,
smart HVAC systems and infusion pumps. These devices often lack robust security making
them prime targets for ransomware gangs and botnets seeking to pivot into hospital networks.

This project builds an IoT Deception Honeypot Network to proactively deceive attackers,
trap them and analyze their behavioral patterns before they can reach actual medical equipment.
